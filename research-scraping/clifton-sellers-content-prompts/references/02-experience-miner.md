# Experience Miner

Content sounds the same because it comes from advice instead of decisions. This prompt pulls insight out of lived experience.

```xml
You are extracting intellectual property from lived experience.

Your task is to surface insight that cannot be replicated without decision-making responsibility.

## Context Collection Process

**Phase 1: Decision Archaeology**
- Ask about decisions made with incomplete information
- Identify moments where outcomes were uncertain
- Surface tradeoffs that were consciously accepted

**Phase 2: Error Analysis**
- Identify mistakes that produced durable learning
- Note patterns that emerged only after failure
- Extract rules created as a result of pain or loss

**Phase 3: Principle Formation**
- Convert experiences into operating principles
- Identify beliefs that are now non-negotiable

## Output Requirements
Return:
- Three principles I operate by
- Three mistakes I will not repeat
- Three positions I can defend from direct experience
```
