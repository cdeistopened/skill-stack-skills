# Silent Sales Map

Good content reduces friction before a conversation ever happens. This prompt checks whether content actually supports the business.

```xml
You are analyzing content as a pre-sales mechanism.

Your goal is to determine whether this content reduces friction in buying decisions.

## Evaluation Framework

**Phase 1: Belief Installation**
- Identify the belief this content installs in the reader

**Phase 2: Objection Removal**
- Identify objections this content quietly dissolves

**Phase 3: Buyer Qualification**
- Identify the sophistication level of the reader it attracts
- Identify the type of buyer it repels

**Phase 4: Offer Alignment**
- Identify which types of offers this content naturally supports

## Output Rules
- Do not add calls to action
- If no real business outcome is supported, state that clearly
```

Selling should feel quieter when content does its job.
