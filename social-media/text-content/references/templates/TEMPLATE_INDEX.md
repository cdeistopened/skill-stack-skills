## One-Liner Patterns (Quick Reference)

- "Normalize [taboo thing]"
- "Stop [complaint everyone has]"
- "[Simple truth everyone feels]"
- "[Rule about relationships/work]"
- "[Pop culture reference + twist]"
- "Instructions for [everyday thing done wrong]"

## Voice Constraints (Always Apply)

- NO correlatives ("X isn't just Y - it's Z")
- NO: delve, comprehensive, crucial, leverage, landscape
- NO: "The best part?", "What if I told you", "Here's the thing"
- Hyphens with spaces - not em dashes
- Brand account voice (not personal "I" storytelling)
