# Keyword Optimization Examples

Before/after examples showing effective keyword strategy by genre.

## Example 1: Christian Non-Fiction (Fasting Book)

**Book:** A 40-day Lenten fasting guide combining Catholic spirituality with modern fasting science

**Title:** "The Benedict Challenge: Recovering the Lost Tradition of One Meal a Day Fasting"

### Before (Common Mistakes)

| Slot | Keywords | Problem |
|------|----------|---------|
| 1 | fasting | Single word, too broad |
| 2 | lent | In title, wasted |
| 3 | benedict | In title, wasted |
| 4 | one meal a day | In subtitle, wasted |
| 5 | christian fasting book | Generic |
| 6 | | Empty slot |
| 7 | | Empty slot |

**Issues:** 3 wasted slots on title words, 2 empty slots, overly generic

### After (Optimized)

| Slot | Keywords (≤50 chars) | Strategy |
|------|----------------------|----------|
| 1 | catholic spiritual discipline lenten devotional | Primary audience |
| 2 | christian intermittent omad diet guide | Health crossover |
| 3 | 40 day prayer journal ash wednesday easter | Timing/season |
| 4 | rule of st benedict monastic spirituality | Historical niche |
| 5 | eat fast feast alternative jason fung fans | Comp readers |
| 6 | ketosis autophagy spiritual growth prayer | Science + spirit |
| 7 | religious holidays worship devotion meditations | Category cementing |

**Improvements:** All 7 slots used, no title duplication, mix of specific + broad, category cementing

## Pattern Recognition

### What works across genres:

| Pattern | Example | Why It Works |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| **Comp authors/books** | "fans of [author]" | Captures similar readers |
| **Specific problems** | "anxiety," "procrastination" | Problem-aware buyers |
| **Age/demographic** | "for men over 50" | Targeted audience |
| **Format descriptors** | "quick read," "audiobook" | Format preferences |
| **Use case** | "gift for," "book club" | Buyer context |
| **Tone/content** | "clean," "no gore," "steamy" | Content expectations |
| **Authority** | "research based," "expert" | Credibility signals |

### Common mistakes across genres:

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Single words | Use 3-5 word phrases |
| Title duplication | Use synonyms and related terms |
| Empty slots | Fill all 7, always |
| Too generic | Add specificity (age, problem, format) |
| Claims ("best") | Remove—prohibited |

---

*Keywords should be refreshed every 3-6 months. What worked at launch may not work 6 months later.*
