# Writing Frameworks

Load this reference when you need pre-writing structure or sentence-craft techniques. These are tools, not rules - use what fits.

## SUCKS Pre-Writing Checklist

Before writing, answer:

- **S - Specific**: Who is the ONE reader? Write for them, not a crowd.
- **U - Unique & Useful**: Does this change how they think, feel, or act? Have they seen this perspective before?
- **C - Clear, Curious, Conversational**: Main idea unmissable. Questions that make readers think. Reads like talking to a friend.
- **K - Kept Simple & Structured**: Simple ideas, simple words. Clear beginning, middle, end.
- **S - Sticky**: Memorable phrases they'll repeat. Ideas that lodge in memory.

Quick check:
- [ ] Who is my ONE reader?
- [ ] What will they think, feel, or do differently?
- [ ] Could a 12-year-old understand the main point?

## Sticky Sentence Techniques

For the 2-3 most important statements in a piece, strengthen with:

**Alliteration** - Same starting sounds
- "Specificity is the secret"
- "The best jobs are neither decreed nor degreed"

**Symmetry** - Parallel structure
- "Read for awareness. Write for understanding."
- "It's not 10,000 hours. It's 10,000 iterations."

**Contrast** - Opposing ideas
- "To be everywhere is to be nowhere."

**Rhythm** - Pleasing cadence
- "When you can't wait to share it, they can't help but read it"

Combine for maximum effect: "Anger prepares us to fight. Fear prepares us to flee." (Symmetry + Alliteration)

## Energy Transfer

The best writing transfers energy from writer to reader.

- Write conversations, not speeches
- Speak WITH your audience, not AT them
- Replace corporate jargon with plain language
- Replace vague claims with specific numbers, examples, names

Test: Would you say this to a friend? If not, rewrite it plainly.

## The Humanization Workflow (Editing AI Output)

1. **Identify the core message** - What's being said that no one else is saying?
2. **Rewrite, don't patch** - Reconstruct from scratch rather than tweaking phrases
3. **Apply hard constraints** - Run through the 12 constraints in the main SKILL.md
4. **Strengthen 2-3 key statements** - Apply sticky sentence techniques
5. **Scan for AI tells** - Check against `forbidden-patterns.md`
6. **Read aloud** - Does it sound like a person thinking or a document performing?
